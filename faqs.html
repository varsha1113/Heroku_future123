FAQ

Why the study of Astrology should be encouraged?

  • It has been studied by some of the keenest intellects in the past. and is still studied by intellectual men in the present.
  • It appeals to the intellectual side of our nature and encourages the active use of mind and thought.
  • It invites and stimulates an honest inquiry into the laws governing the earth and its inhabitants.
  • It enables us to obtain a more correct knowledge of ourselves in our relation to the physical world than any other scientific system of inquiry..
  • It is main entrance into a fuller and more complete understanding of new psychology.
  • It clearly demonstrates to the average mind a wise intelligence behind the manifested universe and reveals a perfect ordering of all the things.
  • It denotes the place in evolution of each individual and indicates the limitations imposed upon him for the purpose of learning the lessons that earth life has to teach.
  • It teaches us whom to respect as true superiors, whom to admit as equals and those whom we have to help as inferiors.
  • It strengthens faith in a divine ruler and soon establishes a conviction that we suffer from ourselves, thus confirming the universal saying that we shall reap as we have sown.
  • It enables us to accept our environment as the best for our immediate purpose, in which our energies may be fruitfully expressed.
  • It enables us to discover with whom we may make profitable acquaintance and how to adapt and adjust our selves to circumstances.
  • It shows where our affections may be safely placed, and also when and where they will be reciprocated.
  • It rationally explains our sympathies and antipathies and why we are magnetically drawn toward some individuals and repelled by others.
  • It quickly proves that there is no such things as a chance or accident and inspires us to see that all is designed for our eventual perfection.
  • It leaves no doubt as to character being destiny, and thus aids us to avoid careless censure of others, but instead produces a better understanding of their actions.
  • It helps us in knowing how to acquire command over our lower nature and control those sensations and vapouring’s likely to hinder our higher evolution.
  • It enables us to trace consequences events and happenings to their legitimate cause and realize that it is not impossible to read the register of our motives.
  • It enables us to forecast the result of certain feelings thoughts and actions and by the use of forethought to turn them to good account and future welfare.
  • It shows us in which direction to look for genius how to use the creative faculties and in which direction to exercise our imagination.
  • It is the best means to removing ignorance from our path and paves the way for much greater enlightment than we have anticipated.
